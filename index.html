About.

A Non-Profit with a goal to encourage and develop private and public routes for ATV/UTV use locally.

Meetings.

Meetings are held on the second Monday of most months, typically from April through October. Everyone is welcome to attend. Members who attend will receive a ticket for a drawing to win one of two $20 gift certificates.

Typical Meeting Agenda:

  • Review of last month's minutes
  • Financial report
  • New business/updates
  • Adjourn
  • Attendance drawing

Membership.

**New session coming soon.**
